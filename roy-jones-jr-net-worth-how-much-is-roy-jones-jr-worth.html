Roy Jones Jr Net Worth: How Much Is Roy Jones Jr Worth?

Roy Jones Jr has an estimated net worth of $7 million. He made this money through his professional career as a boxer, actor, and rapper.

Born in Pensacola, Florida, Jones began his boxing career at an early age. As an amateur, he won the 1984 United States National Junior Olympics and the 1986 and 1987 United States National Golden Gloves.

Jones turned professional in 1989. On May 6, 1989, knocking out Ricky Randall in two rounds in Pensacola at the Bayfront Auditorium.

He subsequently made his first attempt at a world title on May 22, 1993, where beat future Undisputed middleweight champion Bernard Hopkins in Washington, D.C., to attain the IBF middleweight championship.

Since turning pro, Jones competed in several divisions including Heavyweight, Middleweight, Light heavyweight, Super middleweight, and Light middleweight.

After a professional career that spanned nearly three decades, Roy Jones, Jr. retired with a career of 66 wins out of 75 fights. During that career, he earned $55 million from guaranteed purses, Pay Per View bonuses, and sponsors.
