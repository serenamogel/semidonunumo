Career

Mischa Hasnaeni Moein worked in the administration for many years before entering politics. From 2003 until 2006, she was the Commissioner of PT. Mission Mulia Production. In 2009, she also served as Chairman of the National Democratic Party’s National Leadership Council. In 2017, she chose to run for Governor of Makassar, however, she did not win the election. She was the most aspirational candidate for Governor of DKI Jakarta in 2012 and became known as the Golden Woman during the same years. While running for Governor in 2017, Mischa also worked as a daily administrator for the Democratic Party DPP.

Mischa Hasnaeni Moein was a businesswoman before becoming a politician, and she held significant positions in many government areas. She was an entrepreneur in the contracting and energy industries. Mischa has been in this profession since she was 19 years old. She owns the firm PT Misi Mulia Metrical.
