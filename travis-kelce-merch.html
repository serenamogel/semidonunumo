Travis Kelce has taken the world by storm lately and it's not just for his prowess on the football field. The Kansas City Chiefs tight end seemingly has a new title as Taylor Swift's (maybe?) boyfriend. No stranger to sold-out stadiums, Swift was at Kansas City's Arrowhead Stadium perched up in an exclusive suite next to no other than Kelce's mom, Donna Kelce, for the duration of the game. Any time the camera caught a glimpse of Swift she was seen enthusiastically cheering on her man. In fact, cameras spent so much time on Swift's every move that we even got to see what she had to drink and eat during the game. For the record, she ate chicken fingers with ketchup and what was described as "seemingly ranch."
