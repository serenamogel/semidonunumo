From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Ryan Bartley is a voice actress for the English dub of the Pokémon anime. She is currently affiliated with AVO Talent Agency.

Biography

Bartley was born on February 3, 1998, in Forth Worth, Texas, USA, and became a voice actress as a child. She joined the anime during Pokémon Journeys: The Series, where she voiced several characters and Pokémon, her most notable being that of Allister and Goh's Alcremie.
