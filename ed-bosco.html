From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Ed Bosco is an American actor for the English dub of the Pokémon anime.

Biography

Bosco was born on January 24, 1986, in Chicago, Illinois, and became a voice actor as an adult. Bosco first worked on Pokémon by voicing characters in Pokémon Masters EX and Pokémon: Twilight Wings before joining the main series during Pokémon Journeys: The Series. During Journeys, Bosco was credited as Edward Bosco before becoming credited as Ed Bosco in Horizons.
