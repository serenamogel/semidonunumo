    How To Make Beer Cheese Dip

    Beer Cheese Dip

    This easy Beer Cheese Dip is such a crowd pleaser and takes just 5 minutes to make! Made with bacon and a variety of seasonings, it is best served with fresh cut veggies, pretzels, and bread sticks!

    Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword bacon, beer, cheese, dip Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Chill 1 hour hour Servings 10 Calories 173kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 12 oz sharp cheddar cheese grated
    • 1 cup Friendship Dairies cottage cheese
    • 1 garlic clove roughly chopped
    • 1 tsp ground mustard
    • 1/2 tsp onion powder
    • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
    • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 tsp hot sauce optional
    • 1/2 cup beer or ale
    • 3 slices cooked bacon roughly chopped
    • fresh chives thinly sliced, for garnish, optional
    • bacon crumbles for garnish, optional

    Instructions

    • Pour beer into a measuring cup and let it become flat while you gather the remaining ingredients.

    • Add all of the ingredients to food processor in order listed except for the bacon.

    • Pulse a few times to incorporate.

    • Turn food processor to high and puree until nice and smooth or desired consistency is achieved.

    • Add bacon and pulse a few times until it is incorporated.

    • Remove the dip from the processor and place in refrigerator to chill for at least an hour for best flavor.

    • Serve with pretzels, bread sticks, and fresh veggies.
