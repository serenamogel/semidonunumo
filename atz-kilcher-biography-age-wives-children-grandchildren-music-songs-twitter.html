Atz Kilcher Homestead

The Kilcher homestead is located in Alaska outside of Homer. They have lived in their 600-acre land since the early 1940s. Their way of life has been farming, cattle grazing, and coal heating. When he was young, his mother homeschooled him and his siblings until roads were built in their area.
