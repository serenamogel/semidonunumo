MUSIC: Billie Eilish Halleys Comet

Top American singer and Grammy Award winner, Billie Eilish unfolds a new impressive single labeled; “Halley’s Comet” off her just released studio album, “Happier Than Ever”.

Her new album, ‘Happier Than Ever’ was released under the imprint of Darkroom and Interscope Records. It comes after her debut studio album, “When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?” released in 2019.

Billie Eilish has cited self-reflection as the biggest inspiration for the new body of works. She gained prominence in 2015 with the release of her debut single, ‘Ocean Eyes’ followed by her debut extended play, ‘Don’t Smile At Me’ in 2017.

Eilish has received several awards including seven Grammy Awards, two American Music Awards, one Guinness World Records, three MTV Video Music Awards, and two Brit Awards.

She is the youngest artist in Grammy history and second overall to win all four field categories namely; Best New Artist, Record of the Year, Song of the Year, and Album of the Year in the same year.

According to the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) and Billboard, Billie Eilish is one of the most successful artists of the 2010’s decade. She co-wrote and performed ‘No Time To Die’, the theme song for a James Bond film, ‘No Time To Die’.
