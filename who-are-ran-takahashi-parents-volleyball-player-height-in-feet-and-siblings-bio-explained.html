Who Are Ran Takahashi Parents? Volleyball Player Height In Feet And Siblings, Bio Explained

Ran Takahashi’s Parents has been supportive oh his career and even his sibling have followed his footstep and entered the volleyball world.

Ran Takahashi is a Japanese male volleyball player and Olympian who plays for Nippon Sport Science University’s club as an Outside Hitter.

Ran became an internet celebrity after executing a false spike to set the ball for Japanese volleyball team captain Yki Ishikawa to score.

Who Are Ran Takahashi Parents? 

Ran Takahashi has not yet disclosed his parents’ names but we do know his mother is half Japanese and half german meanwhile.

Ran is descended from an American family through his grandfather. Ran was born on September 2, 2001, in the Kyoto region of Japan.

Ran began playing volleyball for the school team in the second grade of elementary school. Ran was a member of the Kyoto Municipal Hachigaoka Junior High School team.

Because of his height, he was mostly utilized as a Libero in first grade. Ran enrolled in Nippon Sport Science University after graduating from high school.

On December 19, he made his first appearance in the top Italian division as a substitute in the third set against Modena Volley.
