FAQs

Why does he have J as a middle name when his real name is Michael Andrew Fox?

When he tried to register for the Screen Actor’s Guild, he found out that the name “Michael Fox” was already taken. So, instead of registering as Michael A. Fox, he registered himself as Michael J. Fox. He was paying homage to actor Michael J. Pollard by placing “J” as a middle name.

And, the next reason for not putting “A” was due to the fact that some magazines and newspapers might use headlines such as “Michael, A Fox!” Hilarious, right?

Does Michael J Fox have Parkinson’s disease?

Michael was diagnosed with young-onset Parkinson’s disease in 1991, at the age of 29.

However, he did not publicly disclose his diagnosis until 1998. He semi-retired from the film industry after being public about his condition.

In the year 2000, the actor established The Michael J . Fox Foundation for Parkinson’s Research.
