Punit Pathak is a multi-skilled person who flaunts his skills as his skills are not just confined to acting, yet he is a well-trained dancer and choreographer as well. He has acted in many movies for which he got much acclaim. Also, he appeared in the reality TV show named “Dance India Dance”. Additionally, he choreographed the famed TV actress Mouni Roy in the reality TV show named “Jhalak Dikhla Jaa”.

Moreover, Punit Pathak performed in a movie named “Anybody Can Dance” in which he appeared as Chandu. The film story revolves around the guy (Chandu) who is a drug addict but dance changes his life and in the end, he dies in a road accident. Apart from acting, he appeared as a Judge on the reality TV show “Dance India Dance 5th season”. Additionally, being a trained choreographer, he choreographed various shows including “Jhalak Dikhla Jaa”, “Dance Ke Superstars”, “DID” and many more.

Once Punit Pathak revealed that he used to take part in various college festivals to hone his dancing skills. During those days, Terence Lewis was looking for young, talented dancers and fortunately, Punit Pathak got selected among 89 participants. Since then, he had been working with Terence Lewis for four years.

However, the young, high-spirited star – Punit Pathak has achieved huge applause and recognition for his amazing dance and choreography skills. Still, he is looking forward to getting some more opportunities.
