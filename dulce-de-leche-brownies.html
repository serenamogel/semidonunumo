Dulce de Leche Brownies

I think these brownies are just gorgeous!  And easy‚Ä¶did I mention easy?

Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword brownies, ducle de leche brownies Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 40 minutes minutes Total Time 45 minutes minutes Servings 24 Calories 112kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 18 oz Ghiradelli Brownie mix 1 box
  • 3/4 cup Dulce de Leche Milk Caramel Spread

Instructions

  • Prepare Brownie mix according to directions. Spread half of it in pan.

  • Heat 3/4 cup of caramel spread in 15 seconds intervals at medium heat in the microwave until the spread loosens.

  • Drop half of the caramel spread by teaspoons on top of the brownie batter and swirl in with a knife. Repeat with the second half of the brownie batter and caramel.

  • Bake according to the package directions.

  • Cool and serve.
